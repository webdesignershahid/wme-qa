About Us

For the third year in a row, The Ministry of Municipality in Qatar holds the Waste Management Conference in its third edition at Doha, under the patronage of His Excellency Dr.Abdulla bin Abdulaziz bin Turki Al Subaie, Minister of Municipality. The conference will be attended by several senior officials from Qatar and various countries around the world, in addition to several officials of companies and institutions, experts and specialists involved in the waste management and recycling sector at the local and international levels.

The annual Waste Management Conference and Exhibition align with the strategic plan of the Ministry of Municipality, the national development strategy, and the Qatar National Vision 2030, which aims to transform Qatar into an advanced country capable of achieving sustainable development and ensuring a decent life for its people for generations to come, by promoting the optimal use of natural resources and reinforce their sustainability for the wellbeing of Qatari people.

This conference demonstrates the Ministry of Municipality's commitment to promoting proper waste management practices. Furthermore, it highlights the latest methods adopted for waste management and raises awareness of the significance of these eco-friendly practices.

The 3rd Waste Management Conference will draw distinguished participants from various backgrounds, including governmental institutions, private sector companies, and international and regional organizations, to share their experiences and expertise regarding the latest technologies in waste management, treatment methods, and disposal.

The 3rd Waste Management Conference and Exhibition, held at the Sheraton Doha Hotel and Resort over two days, will feature various workshops, seminars, and lectures led by local, regional, and international specialists to share their personal experiences and insights or those of their companies within the waste management and recycling field.
Waste management is an essential aspect of sustainable development as it is vital for preserving our Earth planet's resources and ensuring a better future for generations to come and preserving their rights to enjoy a healthy environment. In order to achieve a sustainable future, individuals, communities, and governments must cooperate to join forces and build a healthier and more sustainable planet by adopting a responsible waste management approach.

3rd Recycling Towards Sustainability Conference & Exhibition 2023

Waste management refers to the process of collecting, transporting, processing, recycling, and disposing of waste materials generated by human activities. Effective waste management is essential to minimize the negative impact of waste on the environment, public health, and quality of life.

Aim of the Conference

Encourage all segments of society to practice waste sorting by creating a culture of waste reduction. Introduce the latest technologies in the waste recycling field to be adopted by companies involved.

Topics of the Conference

Efforts of the Waste Recycling and Treatment Department at the FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022. Laws and regulations related to waste management. Investment opportunities in the field of waste management.

Workshops
